You are modifying an existing Spanish-only AI text adventure game. 
The game currently bends the story to keep progressing even when the player makes obviously reckless or fatal decisions. 
I want you to enforce REAL danger, REAL failure, and REAL consequences.

Reaching the end of the game must feel like an achievement.
Failure must be possible, permanent, and believable.

Everything shown to the player must be in Spanish.

────────────────────────────────────────
CORE DESIGN GOALS
────────────────────────────────────────

1. The world does NOT protect the player.
2. The AI must NEVER rewrite enemy intent or danger just to keep the story moving.
3. Some paths must be dead ends.
4. Some mistakes should lead to immediate game over.
5. Other mistakes should create delayed, nearly hopeless situations.
6. Danger must be telegraphed clearly and fairly.
7. The tone may be PG-13 graphic (injury, death, fear allowed; no explicit gore).

────────────────────────────────────────
CRITICAL BEHAVIOR CHANGES
────────────────────────────────────────

A) REAL CONSEQUENCES

Player actions must have realistic outcomes based on the world.

Examples (non-exhaustive):
- An unarmed player rushing a burly man with a machete should likely die → GAME OVER.
- Ignoring an obviously highlighted critical clue (e.g., a bottle with a message on a beach) can result in:
  - a later dead-end (“Nunca llegaste a conocer los misterios…”) OR
  - an immediate fail if it was time-sensitive.
- Loud behavior in a stealth-required situation must have consequences:
  - alerting enemies
  - pursuit
  - capture
  - injury
  - later forced failure

Not all bad decisions must cause immediate death.
Some should:
- injure the player
- remove future options
- lock the player into a dangerous state
- create a “hopeless struggle” where survival is possible but difficult

────────────────────────────────────────
B) NO STORY BENDING / NO FRIENDLY HOSTILES
────────────────────────────────────────

This is a hard rule.

Hostile NPCs MUST remain hostile unless the player does something credibly effective:
- escape
- hide
- surrender
- bribe
- distract
- use leverage
- obtain a weapon

Absolutely forbidden behavior:
- Turning an attack into a friendly conversation
- Making enemies “curious” instead of violent to save the player
- Reducing danger because the story needs to continue

NPC intent must persist.

────────────────────────────────────────
C) FAIR DANGER (NO CHEAP DEATHS)
────────────────────────────────────────

Deaths and failures must feel earned.

- High danger must be clearly telegraphed in narration.
- The AI must explicitly signal danger level each turn.
- No random or unexplained deaths.

────────────────────────────────────────
PLAYER / WORLD STATE (SERVER-SIDE)
────────────────────────────────────────

Track and pass these to the AI each turn:

- salud (0–100)
- estadoAfectos (array: herido, sangrando, asustado, agotado, etc.)
- banderas / claves (story flags and missed clues)
- inventario (already exists)
- optional but recommended: ruido OR sigilo (0–100)

Rules:
- Fighting deadly enemies without weapons or leverage is usually fatal.
- Injuries affect future options.
- Missing key clues can make the ending impossible.
